    2. A manager builds and retains a great team which embodies our coffee shop's culture by providing excellent customer service and the best standards of coffee and food at all times.

  2. Coffee Barista

    3. A Barista creates and serves specialty hot and cold coffee according to the menu and guests’ special requests. A Barista is knowledgeable about all coffee menu items, provides high-quality, fast-paced service.

    Key Responsibilities:

    • Prepares and serves coffee and other café drinks.
    • Provides guests with exceptional service and product details, such as coffee blend or preparation description.
    • Weighs, grinds or packs coffee beans for guests according to their specifications.
    • Cleans and sanitizes work areas in accordance with supervising health agency of the area.
    • Performs other duties as required or assigned.
